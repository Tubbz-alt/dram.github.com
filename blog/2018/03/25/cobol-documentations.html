作为一个历史悠久的编程语言，COBOL的文档总体还是丰富的，但有些散乱，以下是整理的一些资源，可作入门使用，建议依序阅读。

  • Fujitsu COBOL85 Reference Manual V2.3

  • GnuCOBOL 2.2 NOV2017 Programmers Guide

  • ISO/IEC JTC 001/SC 22/WG 04 "COBOL"

另外以下有一些历史文档，也可供参考：

  • COBOL: Initial Specifications for a COmmon Business Oriented Language, 1960.

  • COBOL: Revised Specifications for a COmmon Business Oriented Language, 1961.

  • COBOL: Extended Specifications for a COmmon Business Oriented Language, 1962.

  • COBOL, A Self-Instructional Manual, 1963.

  • COBOL, 1965.

  • CODASYL COBOL Journal of Development, 1968.
