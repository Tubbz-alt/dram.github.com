使用Matrix中的RSS Bot

02 Jul 2018

Matrix的Go-NEB中包含有RSS功能，具体配置如下：

services:
  - ID: "rss_service"
    Type: "rssbot"
    UserID: "@USER_NAME:SERVER_NAME"
    Config:
      feeds:
        "https://foo.bar/baz.rss":
          rooms: ["!ROOM_NAME:SERVER_NAME"]
          poll_interval_mins: 120
